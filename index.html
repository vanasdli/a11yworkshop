Digital Accessibility 101 Exercises

What follows are a series of short, informational pages that each include a 3-4 forms of inaccessibility. Your goal is to identify what is inaccessible about each page and how it could be improved.

The purpose of this exercise is to give participants the opportunity to work with an inaccessible website in order to identify pain points and suggest solutions.
